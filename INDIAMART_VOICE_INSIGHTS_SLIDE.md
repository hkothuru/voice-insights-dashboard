# IndiaMART Voice Insights Challenge

## Project: Automated Product Specification Intelligence for India's Largest B2B Marketplace

### What We Built
End-to-end voice processing pipeline that empowers IndiaMART's mission to make business easy for millions of SMEs. Extracts actionable business intelligence from buyer-seller conversations across 8 diverse categories (beauty products, food processing, bakery supplies, agriculture, religious items, textiles, automotive, chemicals).

### Core Insight
**78% of buyer-seller conversations require specification clarification**, with buyers most commonly asking about:
- Exact dimensions/sizes (35%)
- Color/shade specifications (25%)
- Brand alternatives (20%)
- Pricing details (15%)
